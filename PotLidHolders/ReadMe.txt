3d printed parametric Pot Lid Holders for your kitchen organizing needs!
Included several fun patterns & designs.

Designed in Fusion360
Utilizes "Parameter I/O" AddOn to easily customize dimensions.
The included .csv files fit my pots/pans with lid diameter of 5.375 in, 6.25 in, etc.

Printing Instructions:
3 walls, 10% infill, print vertically (see PrintPreview.jpg), some patterns may require supports
